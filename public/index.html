About ISRM

Our Mission

Build an interactive, wiki-like resource for petrol scooter repair and maintenance—similar to ETKA for Volkswagen but with 3D models, videos, and dynamic content.

Content Updates

ISRM automatically syncs daily with trusted sources: professional repair sites, manufacturer resources, YouTube tutorials, and verified community forums.

Our Focus

MVP coverage includes GY6-engined scooters and Piaggio models. We prioritize accuracy, verification, and community contributions.

Always Verify

All procedures should be verified with your scooter's official manual. ISRM provides guidance but users are responsible for safe execution.
