About ISMR

Why ISMR?

The inspiration is ETKA — the legendary Volkswagen parts and repair catalogue that makes finding the right information fast and reliable. ISMR aims to be the equivalent for petrol scooter owners: structured, searchable, and always improving. What ETKA is for VW technicians, ISMR aspires to be for GY6 and Piaggio/Vespa riders.

Scope & Sources

ISMR covers GY6-engined scooters (50–150 cc) and Piaggio/Vespa models — Zip, Primavera, and Sprint. Content is sourced from official service manuals, verified YouTube channels, and professional repair communities. Every guide cites its sources so you can go deeper if needed.

Always Verify

All procedures should be verified against your scooter's official service manual before you start work. ISMR provides guidance based on available sources, but you are responsible for safe execution — torque specs, fluid specs, and clearances can differ between model years and markets.

How It's Built

ISMR was built by a non-developer using AI as architect, developer, and reviewer — from blank page to live site in a matter of days.

About the Creator

I am a scooter repair enthusiast building this site for fun in my spare time alongside a full-time career in Project and Delivery Management. During my own learning journey I kept running into the same problem: reliable repair information is scattered across Reddit threads, old forums, and hundreds of YouTube videos. I have gradually gathered knowledge that could help others and simplify that search — so I decided to turn it into a free, organised resource. Because I am not a professional mechanic, some content may not be 100% mechanically accurate. I do my best to verify everything against official service manuals, and corrections are always welcome.
