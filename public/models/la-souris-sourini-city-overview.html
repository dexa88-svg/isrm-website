La Souris Sourini / City S — Model Overview

Added: 2026-05-07  |  Sources: ScooterForum.net, Retroscooteronderdelen.nl, La Souris Euro 5 owner log  |  models La Souris GY6 50cc
📌 About La Souris: La Souris ("The Mouse" in French) is a Dutch-market scooter brand. The Sourini and City S are retro-styled 50cc snorscooters sold primarily in the Netherlands and Belgium. They use a GY6-based 50cc engine platform and share many components with other Chinese-made scooters including the BTC Riva, AGM VX50, and Senzo Rivalux.

Known Issues & Common Repairs

Cold-Start Problems

The most frequently reported issue on Dutch scooter forums is difficulty starting in cold or damp weather. The root causes are typically the auto-choke thermostatic element becoming sluggish in temperatures below 5°C, a partially blocked pilot jet, or a slightly low float level. See the GY6 Carburetor Adjustment Guide and GY6 No-Start Troubleshooting for systematic diagnosis.

Carburetor Type

La Souris Sourini and City S carbureted models typically use a Rongmao or Keihin PD18J clone carburetor. Pilot jet size is typically 35–38 and main jet is typically 72–78 for the restricted 25 km/h snorfiets version.

Lighting

All La Souris City models have conventional halogen bulbs accessible through an access flap behind the headlamp — no special tools required for bulb replacement. City S models from 2019 onwards may have LED daytime running lights combined with halogen low beam.

Parts Interchangeability

The La Souris Sourini and City S share the GY6 platform with several other European-market Chinese scooters. The following models are mechanically similar and share most engine and drivetrain parts:

  • BTC Riva (same engine, same CVT belt, same GY6 carburetor)
  • AGM VX50
  • Senzo Rivalux
  • GTS Toscana
  • Generic Chinese 50cc scooters marketed under many brand names

Body panels, headlamp units, mirrors, and instruments are not interchangeable between these brands — each has its own styling. Engine internals (pistons, camshafts, valves, bearings, seals), CVT components (rollers, belt, variator), and electrical components (CDI, rectifier, ignition coil) are largely interchangeable as they all derive from the same GY6 platform.

💡 Parts sourcing tip: In the Netherlands and Belgium, chinascooteronderdelen.nl specifically stocks parts for La Souris, BTC Riva, AGM, and Senzo models. For generic GY6 engine parts, any supplier stocking 139QMB parts will work.
