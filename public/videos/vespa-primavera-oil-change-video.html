This curated video collection covers the oil change procedure for modern Vespa scooters — primarily the Primavera, Sprint, and GTS models — all powered by Piaggio's fuel-injected iGet engine family. These are beginner-friendly DIY procedures that require only basic tools and take 20–30 minutes.

Main Video

What This Video Covers

  • Locating the drain plug and oil filler cap on iGet-engine Vespa scooters
  • Correct oil specification for Primavera and Sprint (5W-40 full synthetic, JASO MA2)
  • Drain plug copper washer replacement
  • Correct torque for the drain plug: 22 Nm
  • Correct oil fill volume: 800 ml (125 cc) or 900 ml (150 cc)
  • Checking the dipstick level after refill
  • Resetting the service reminder light on the Vespa dashboard
💡 Compatible models: This procedure applies to Vespa Primavera 125, Primavera 150, Sprint 125, Sprint 150 (all years 2013–present), Vespa GTS 125, GTS 150, GTS 300 (iGet engine versions from approx. 2019 onwards). Earlier GTS models (2005–2018) with the HPE/Super 300 engine have a slightly different procedure — consult the model-specific service manual.
