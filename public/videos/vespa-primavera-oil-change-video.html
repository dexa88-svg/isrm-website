DIY Vespa Oil Change — Primavera / GTS / Sprint

Added: 2026-05-11 | Source: YouTube | Difficulty: Easy | Time: 20–30 min
Piaggio Vespa Primavera Sprint GTS iGet Oil Change
📌 Applies to: Vespa Primavera 125/150, Sprint 125/150 (2013–present), Vespa GTS 125/150/300 (iGet engine variants, approx. 2019 onwards). Earlier GTS models (2005–2018) with the HPE/Super 300 engine have a slightly different procedure — consult the model-specific service manual.

This curated video covers the oil change procedure for modern Vespa scooters — primarily the Primavera, Sprint, and GTS models powered by Piaggio's fuel-injected iGet engine. A beginner-friendly DIY job requiring only basic tools and taking 20–30 minutes.

What This Video Covers

  • Locating the drain plug and oil filler cap on iGet-engine Vespa scooters
  • Correct oil specification for Primavera and Sprint (5W-40 full synthetic, JASO MA2)
  • Drain plug copper washer replacement
  • Correct torque for the drain plug: 22 Nm
  • Correct oil fill volume: 800 ml (125 cc) or 900 ml (150 cc)
  • Checking the dipstick level after refill
  • Resetting the service reminder light on the Vespa dashboard
