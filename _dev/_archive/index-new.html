About ISRM

Interactive Scooter Repair Manuals is a free, community-driven resource for electric scooter owners, mechanics, and enthusiasts. We aggregate repair guides, technical documentation, video tutorials, and user wisdom from trusted sources worldwide—including professional repair sites, manufacturers, YouTube creators, and verified community forums.

Our mission is to make scooter repair knowledge accessible, interactive, and continuously updated—similar to ETKA for Volkswagen vehicles, but designed specifically for electric scooters with features like 3D part visualizations, video embeds, and collaborative community contributions.

We're building: A wiki-like repair database that stays current with new models and firmware updates, a searchable parts compatibility chart, interactive 3D diagrams, and eventually a mobile app for repairs on-the-go.
