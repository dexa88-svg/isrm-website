Community Guidelines

  1. Be Respectful: Treat all community members with courtesy and respect. No harassment, discrimination, or offensive behavior.
  2. Stay On Topic: Keep discussions related to scooter repair, maintenance, and enthusiasts. Use appropriate forums for different topics.
  3. Verify Information: Always verify repairs against official manuals before attempting. ISMR is a guide, not a substitute for professional service.
  4. Share Knowledge: Help others by sharing your experience. Ask questions respectfully and provide complete information when posting problems.
  5. No Spam: Don't post promotional content, commercial links, or spam. Community is for peer support only.
  6. Safety First: Always prioritize safety. If you're unsure about a procedure, ask for help or consult a professional mechanic.
  7. Contribute Verified Content: Share guides and tips, but ensure they're accurate and tested. Incorrect information harms the community.
  8. Respect Moderators: Moderators keep the community safe and helpful. Respect their decisions and follow their guidance.
